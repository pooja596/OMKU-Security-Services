OMKU security services is the pioneer in the field of security service. OMKU security services is a elite Private Security Agency licensed by the government of Maharashtra headquartered at Shelar, Dist. Thane, with a full fledged training centre at Shelar, Dist.Thane. The main objective of the OMKU are armor of life and property maintenance of order and peace in the work place and neighborhood prevention of sabotage by anti-social and anti -national elements and assisting the uniformed guards in alleviating the after month of natural disaster during emergency situation or during big religious of order celebration . OMKU security force services has a full-fledged and well equipped training centre where all the guards imparted essential training required to perform the security job. Training curriculum includes industrial security, fire fighting , disaster management , intelligence collection , traffic and parking management , unarmed combat tactics , liaison with police and other uniformed forces.

Services

Wide spectrum of service will be provided by OMKU which will include integrated security solution for banks, financial intuitions, ware houses , mail ,theaters, hotels, rich and farm house and public figures like politicians , businessmen , jewelry shop , mass transportation systems such as railway , large gathering places such as stadiums etc. by providing security guards and body guards and internal communication system installation by radio system and digital security system installation.

Corporate Service Guards

Corporate security guards are employed for the purpose of ensuring corporate security for your business. The corporate security personnel are also responsible for safety of the resources, information and people.

Armed Security guards

As the armed suggests the armed security guard service provides the clients with the security guard who is armed. They are mostly employed for the persons whose life and property is in great danger. This is because the armed guards are highly skilled in using weapons.

Dog Scode

Dog squad with handlers is deployed considering the location, situation and requirement. These are special dogs bred and selected for detecting explosives and contraband. Under the supervision of trained handlers these dogs, usually Labradors orAlsatians, can protect a large area from bomb threats and other such dangers.

Personal body guards and bouncers

Our body guards/ bouncers are trained to act discrete and blend in with the environment. We will protect you at home, on your every day life and vacation.

Residential sercurity Guards

Like the types of security personnel, the residential security officers are also responsible for the safety of the clients. They are usually employed in apartments, center for the elderly and other residential areas.
