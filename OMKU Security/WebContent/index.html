What we are ?

OMKU security services is the pioneer in the field of security service. OMKU security services is a elite Private Security Agency licensed by the government of Maharashtra headquartered at Shelar, Dist. Thane, with a full fledged training centre at Shelar, Dist.Thane.

The main objective of the OMKU are armor of life and property maintenance of order and peace in the work place and neighborhood prevention of sabotage by anti-social and anti -national elements and assisting the uniformed guards in alleviating the after month of natural disaster during emergency situation or during big religious of order celebration.

OMKU security force services has a full-fledged and well equipped training centre where all the guards imparted essential training required to perform the security job. Training curriculum includes industrial security, fire fighting , disaster management , intelligence collection , traffic and parking management , unarmed combat tactics , liaison with police and other uniformed forces.
